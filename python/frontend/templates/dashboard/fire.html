What is FIRE?

FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. It's a movement focused on building enough wealth so your investments generate enough passive income to cover your living expenses — giving you the freedom to stop working for money.

FIRE Number

The corpus you need. Typically 25× your annual expenses (based on the 4% safe withdrawal rule).

How It Works

Save aggressively (50-70% of income), invest in equities + debt, let compounding do the work over 10-20 years.

Why It Matters

Freedom to pursue passions, travel, or simply not worry about money. You work because you want to, not because you have to.
